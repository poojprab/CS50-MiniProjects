APPLE PIE


INGREDIENTS

Pie Crust

Apples

Brown + Granulated Sugar

Sea Salt

Cinnamon

Ginger

Cardamom

All Spice

Nutmeg

Flour

Butter

Egg

RECIPE

To make the filling, we toss apples with a mixture of sugars, cardamom, allspice, cinnamon, salt, and a little nutmeg. Then we add the eggs, butter, and flour. When everything is well tossed, we tumble the filling into a prepared crust.

We cut butter (or other solid fat) into flour until the butter and flour look crumbly and have pieces of butter the size of peas. Then, we add just enough water to form the dough into a ball
