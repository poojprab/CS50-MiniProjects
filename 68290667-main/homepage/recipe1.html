BLUEBERRY PIE


INGREDIENTS

Pie Crust

Blueberries

Sugar

Lemon Zest

Cinnamon

All Spice

Cornstarch

Buttter

Egg Yolk

Cream

RECIPE

To make the filling, we toss blueberries with a mixture of sugar, cornstarch, allspice, cinnamon, salt, and a little lemon zest. When everything is well tossed, we tumble the filling into a prepared crust.

We cut butter (or other solid fat) into flour until the butter and flour look crumbly and have pieces of butter the size of peas. Then, we add just enough water to form the dough into a ball
